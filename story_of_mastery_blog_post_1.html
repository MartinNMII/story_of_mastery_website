Transformation Happens at the Level of Thought

The vicious cycle:

  1. Thought
  2. Emotion
  3. Action

Emotions are best felt. It's best to allow them to pass through the body. They are changeable.

To the greatest extent, change (improvement, kaizen) happens at the level of THOUGHT and ACTION.

Stories are composed of thoughts. Perspectives are composed of thoughts. Self-image is composed of thoughts.

Certainly, thoughts are an integral part of every process. However, relying on thoughts is best when change is in question. This brings us to the INNER GAME.

The inner game is a combination of thoughts and emotions. The inner game plays from inside out. Inner change creates outer change. The outer game plays from outside in. External change creates inner change. This is the vicious cycle of success or failure.
